Formal Letter Writing: Structure & Conventions

1. The Sender's Address

The sender's address is usually put on the top right-hand corner of the page.

2. The Date

The date is put below the sender's address on the right-hand side.

3. The Recipient's Address

The recipient's address is put on the left-hand side below the sender's address level.
